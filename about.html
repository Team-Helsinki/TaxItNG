About Taxit.NG

It's a given that governmental performance plays a key role in the willingness of the citizens to continue fulfilling their obligations to the state, one of which includes paying taxes. But when there's a disconnect between service delivery on the government's part and the amount being taxed, what should be an integral part of a state's development then becomes something citizens are wary of. A group of teeming young interns saw this problem and sought to create a portal where there's a continuum between how citizens can assess the government's performance and their resolve to continue paying taxes towards these visible performance improvements. This birthed TaxItNG, a transparent channel through which citizens can hold their government accountable, have the capacity to choose what projects their taxes should go into and effectively track their preferred project's progress. The result, which is our goal, is a change in the prevailing perception of tax payment.

Our Goal

Our goal at TaxIt.NG is to make tax payment seamless and transparent using technology to create a transparent channel through which citizens can hold their government accountable, have the capacity to choose what projects their taxes should go into and effectively track their preferred project's progress to drive the change we need and deserve in Nigeria. We are on a mission to make tax payment seamless and accountable.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to make tax payment seamless and accountable.To reorient the general perception of tax payments through transparency, participation, and accountability.
